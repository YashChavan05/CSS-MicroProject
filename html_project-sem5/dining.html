Dining and drinks

Enjoy a selection of Asian cuisine at 3 Spices. Level 12 boasts panoramic views of the city and mouth-watering North Indian dishes. Miyuki is our Japanese restaurant serving sushi and bento boxes to takeaway. Grab coffee and pastries at Tarta.

3 Spices

Dine in style in the interactive, all-day dining restaurant, Three Spices, where you can choose from either a varied buffet selection or from the a tempting a la carte menu, both featuring local and international specialties and an extensive selection of wines and spirits.


Level 12

It boasts panoramic views of the city and mouth-watering North Indian dishes including savoury and sweet items.

Myuki

Immerse yourself in an authentic Japanese dining experience in our exclusive Japanese restaurant, Miyuki. Savor Teppanyaki, sushi and sashimi, expertly prepared by our skilled chef, or order one of our traditional bento boxes to take-away.

Tarta

Indulge in a delicious, handmade cake or pastry accompanied by a freshly brewed cup of coffee or a pot of tea in Tarta.
